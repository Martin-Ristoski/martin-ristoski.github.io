About The Hobbies:

Through my life I've tried many hobbies. In some I was good, but in some I was trash. I stuck with the ones that I was good in, like my main hobby-cycling. My main discipline is Mountain Biking, but also do some Road Cycling on the side. Sometimes I record my rides, and when I do, I make a short video of it with some editing magic. I didn't know how to edit, but with some help from YouTube I learnt to do, and before I knew it, it turned into a hobby. You can check my latest cycling video here. As I wanted to stay in shape in the winter when I couldn't ride, I got into running and now i occasionally run when I am bored with the bike.
