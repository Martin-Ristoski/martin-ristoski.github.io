I am Martin Ristoski from Tetovo.

Teenage Years:

I ended up choosing SUGS "Orce Nikolov" as my high school, where I graduated in 2021. As I was from a different city, my teens were filled with different, unknown people, so I can freely say that it made me get out of my comfort zone and meet new people from which I learnt a lot and upgraded as a person soaking a bit of knowledge from everyone. In those four years I tried many hobbies, but found myself the most in cycling. After graduating from high school, I chose the Faculty of Software Engineering and Computer Sciences (FINKI) as my college where I am currently studying.
